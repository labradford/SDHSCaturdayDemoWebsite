    Why Adopt an Adult Cat?

  • Adopting an adult cat is a low-cost, low-maintenance, miracle solution to improve your physical, emotional and psychological health.
  • Cat companions can lower your blood pressure, improve your emotional well-being, and bring a positive outlook on life.
  • Your feline friend will add meaning to your life and give you a sense of purpose - without exorbitant costs or huge time commitments.
  • Cats are the purr-fect companion to those who don’t have the time to commit to a pet with high demands.
  • Cats groom and walk themselves, making them the ideal choice for the busy family.
  • Best of all, adopting an adult cat WILL SAVE A LIFE!
