Lulu Bean's Story

"We adopted Louise (aka our little Lulu Bean) this past December. She is now 10 months old and has brought nothing but joy into our lives! We've both never had a cat before and she is definitely the best kitten we could have chosen. Her activity level definitely fits us. She is friendly, cuddly, playful and is small enough to look like our 'permanent kitten!' We love her! Not a day goes by since we've had her that we don't look at her with awe and wonder how we ever lived without her. Thank you for giving us the opportunity to have our forever friend and companion!"

Butterscotch's Story

Happy adoption story…21 years later! “This photo is my 3-year old granddaughter and my orange tabby, Butterscotch. Back in 1994 I found Butterscotch at San Diego Humane Society when she was just 3 months old. I had stopped in looking for a Calico, but there was this tiny orange tabby climbing the wire screen in the cat kennel, meowing plaintively, which translated to ‘take me home with you pleeeeeeeeaaaaaassssseee!!!’ So I changed my mind about the Calico and brought her home, where one of my kitties, Snowflake, taught her how to sleep in my bed and another, Jasmine, taught her how to play!

Butterscotch will be 21 years old in April. She’s probably the best spur of the moment decision I ever made…”

    Why Adopt an Adult Cat?

  • Adopting an adult cat is a low-cost, low-maintenance, miracle solution to improve your physical, emotional and psychological health.
  • Cat companions can lower your blood pressure, improve your emotional well-being, and bring a positive outlook on life.
  • Your feline friend will add meaning to your life and give you a sense of purpose - without exorbitant costs or huge time commitments.
  • Cats are the purr-fect companion to those who don’t have the time to commit to a pet with high demands.
  • Cats groom and walk themselves, making them the ideal choice for the busy family.
  • Best of all, adopting an adult cat WILL SAVE A LIFE!
